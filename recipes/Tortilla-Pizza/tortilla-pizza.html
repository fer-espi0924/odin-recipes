Delicious Tortilla Pizza

A crispy tortilla pizza with all your favorite pizza toppings!

A crispy tortilla pizza is an easier and healthier alternative to a traditional pizza, with all your favorite pizza toppings! Perfect for lunch or dinner. These personal pizzas can be customized to your liking, and they only take a few minutes to make!

Ingredients

  • 2 Flour Tortillas. You'll need 8 to 10-inch, burrito-sized tortillas.
  • 1/2 cup Pizza Sauce. Marinara is a delicious alternative. Either way, divide this into 2 portions.
  • 1/2 cup Mozzarella. Shred it up, and divide it into 2 portions.
  • 1/4 cup Parmesan Cheese. Treat this the same as the mozzarella, shredded and divided.
  • 24 slices Pepperoni. You can always add more depending on the size of your pepperoni. Divide these into 2 portions.

Now the chemical process:

There are only three easy steps! Get out a baking sheet, a pizza cutter, and a wooden spoon to begin.

  1. Prep your oven and your dough. Preheat your oven first. It should be set to 425℉ (218℃). Move the rack to the center of the oven and place a large baking sheet on it as the oven heats. Then, lay out 2 flour tortillas on a clean workspace. Smear 1/4 cup pizza sauce over each.
  2. Add your toppings. Next, spread about 1/4 cup mozzarella cheese on each tortilla. Then, do the same with ¼ cup each of Parmesan cheese. Top it with 12 slices of pepperoni each. Sprinkle on some garlic salt to taste, if you'd like.
  3. Bake, slice, and serve. Transfer the assembled pizzas to the hot baking sheet and cook for approximately 5 minutes. Take it out when the cheese is melted and the tortillas are crisp. Let it cool for a couple of minutes. Then, slice and serve.
